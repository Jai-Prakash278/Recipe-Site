Nutrition

Notes

For larger drumsticks, more cook time might be needed.
For extra large drumsticks I recommend finishing your chicken in the oven at 400°F for 10-15 minutes and then removing them and tenting them with foil and letting them rest for an additional 10 minutes.
If the skin is browning too quickly on your drumsticks then lower the heat a bit.
